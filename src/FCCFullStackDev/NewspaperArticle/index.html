Mysterious Muggle Makes Magic Meringues

A muggle has inadvertently created healing potion meringues

by Mundungus Fletcher

In a bizarre twist that has left the wizarding community both baffled and amused, a Muggle baker in London has inadvertently created what appears to be a batch of healing potion-infused meringues. The confections, initially baked for a local charity event, were found to have remarkable restorative properties. Witnesses report that after consuming the meringues, minor ailments such as headaches and fatigue vanished almost instantly.

The Ministry of Magic has dispatched a team of Obliviators and Potions experts to investigate the incident. Preliminary findings suggest that the Muggle, unaware of the magical properties of her ingredients, may have stumbled upon a rare combination of herbs and essences with potent healing qualities. “It’s highly unusual for a Muggle to accidentally brew something so effective,” remarked Potions Master Horace Slughorn. “We’re looking into whether this was a fluke or if there’s more to it.”

While the Muggle in question remains oblivious to her accidental foray into potion-making, the wizarding world is abuzz with speculation. Could this be a sign of untapped magical potential in the non-magical community? Or is it simply a case of serendipity? For now, the Ministry has confiscated the remaining meringues for further study, leaving the Muggle baker with nothing but a puzzled expression and a very clean kitchen.
