GCN Granola Bars Recipe

The fabled GCN granola bars recipe for all your endurance exercise goodness.

Ingredients

  • 800g coconut oil
  • 250ml maple syrup
  • 1kg rolled oats
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 500g assorted nuts and seeds

Creates 12 bars

Instructions

  1. Mix wet ingredients together
  2. Mix dry ingredients together
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  4. Mix all ingredients together
  5. Spread on a baking tray and bake for 30mins
